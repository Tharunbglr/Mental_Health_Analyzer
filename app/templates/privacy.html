Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

This mental health analysis tool collects the following types of information:

  • Responses to questionnaires (PHQ-9 and GAD-7)
  • Basic demographic information (age)
  • Lifestyle information (sleep, exercise, etc.)
  • Optional free-text notes

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Generate immediate feedback and suggestions
  • Provide crisis resources when needed
  • If consented, generate AI-assisted insights (anonymized)

Data Protection

  • We do not store your responses persistently
  • All analysis is performed in-memory
  • Free-text entries are sanitized before AI processing
  • No personally identifiable information is shared with third parties

AI Processing

If you opt in to AI-assisted insights:

  • Data is anonymized before processing
  • Free text is limited to 500 characters
  • Names and identifiable details are removed
  • Processing is done via secure API endpoints

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of AI processing
  • Request information about your data
  • Request deletion of your data
